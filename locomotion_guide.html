4.1 ABP_3P_LowerBody_For1P — Deep Analysis

Source: Extracted from original game via dump-json "ABP_3P_LowerBody_For1P"
Path: BluePrints/Characters/AnimationBP/Player1P/ABP_3P_LowerBody_For1P

Delta Force uses a separate AnimBP for 1P lower body that runs on the 3P skeleton (C_Test_001_3P_Skeleton, 113 bones). This is independent from the 1P upper body AnimBP (83 bones).

How it works:

1P View Rendering: +---------------------------+ | 1P Upper Mesh | ABP_Character_1P (83 bones) | (M_Kai_Upper_1P) | -> Arms + Weapon visible | arms, hands, weapon | +---------------------------+ + +---------------------------+ | 3P Body Mesh (lower) | ABP_3P_LowerBody_For1P (113 bones) | (M_Kai_Body_3P) | -> Only legs rendered | legs, feet | -> Upper body hidden/masked +---------------------------+ Note: In Delta Force, lower body is NOT visible in 1P. The ABP exists but legs are hidden. Only shadows are cast. For our implementation, we can choose to show or hide legs.
